Welcome

The Carleton University Math Society is a student run organization dedicated to bringing together students and faculty from the School of Mathematics at Carleton. We provide social opportunities for students of all years to form new friendships, and we organize enrichment opportunities that encourage enthusiasm for mathematics. You can look forward to lots of great events. Join our Discord server for more details. Also

You can contact us by email at cumathsoc@gmail.com by joining our Discord Server to stay up to date with all our upcoming events.
